Simple Oatmeal Cookies

Ingredients list

  • 300 g of oatmeal
  • 4 tablespoons flour
  • 160 g butter
  • 8 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • lemon peel
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 150 g raisins

    • Steps for cooking recipe

    1. Sift oatmeal in a pan and toast half of the butter.
    2. Rest of butter, whipped with sugar.
    3. Add grated leamon peel
    4. Add whipped egg, keep for some time at room temperature and mix
    5. Sift and mix the baking powder with the flour. Add to the mixture
    6. Stir in the cooled oatmeal
    7. Finally, stir in the raisins
    8. Place the dough with two teaspoons on a baking pan lined with baking sheet. Bake in a preheated oven at 180 degrees for about 20 minutes - until the biscuits have turned light brown.
